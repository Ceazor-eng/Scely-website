About us

Scely insurance agency is a duly registered and licensed independent insurance agency operating in Kenya.
Being a local independent insurance agency is how we fulfill our passion of serving people. Over the years in the insurance industry, we have saved individuals, businesses and families from financial distress through the various insurance products we offer.
As the insurance industry undergoes digital disruption, affecting how insurers engage with customers, we have re-aligning our products and services to meet emerging consumer trends and expectations in a rapidly digitized business environment
To most people insurance jargons and processes are complex and confusing. However, our agency simplifies things for you. We are good listeners, we answer all your queries ensuring that you have all the information you need to make wise financial decisions.
In the unfortunate event of a loss, we offer unlimited support to our clients by guiding them through the entire claims process. Even in the most complex of situations, we advocate for our clients because our customers are our first priority not the insurance companies we work with.




Mission

To offer our clients all rounded insurance solutions.

Vision

To be the leading go to agency for cost-efficient tailor-made insurance solutions.

Value Preposition

Offer risk averse insurance solutions and protection for your goods, sales, services, staff, assets, and inventory movable and immovable, stationary and on transit.
